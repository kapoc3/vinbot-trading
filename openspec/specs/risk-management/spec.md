## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Real-time Signal Validation
The system SHALL validate every trade signal against risk parameters before execution.

#### Scenario: Block Trade due to Daily Loss
- **GIVEN** the bot has already lost 5% of the total balance today
- **AND** the MAX_DAILY_LOSS is set to 5%
- **WHEN** a BUY signal is generated by the RSI strategy
- **THEN** the system SHALL block the execution and log a "Risk Management: Daily Limit Reached" message.

### Requirement: Automatic Exit (Stop Loss / Take Profit)
The system SHALL monitor the current price against the average entry price for all open positions.

#### Scenario: Trigger Stop Loss
- **GIVEN** an open position for BTCUSDT with entry price 50,000
- **AND** STOP_LOSS_PCT is 2% (Threshold: 49,000)
- **WHEN** the current market price hits or drops below 49,000
- **THEN** the system SHALL immediately execute a SELL MARKET order.
